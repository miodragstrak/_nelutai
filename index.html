About NelutAI
Welcome to NelutAI, your personalized AI-powered travel buddy designed to make your travels smoother and more enriching. We harness the power of open data and artificial intelligence to provide real-time insights on local attractions, events, and more, helping you to experience each city like a local.
How It Works
Our chatbot integrates seamlessly with popular messaging platforms such as WhatsApp, Viber, Telegram, and Facebook Messenger. Just send a message to NelutAI, and instantly receive personalized recommendations based on open data from various sources including statistical offices and open data portals.
Features
Real-Time Insights:
Get the latest information on local attractions, events, and safety conditions.
Personalized Itineraries:
Use machine learning algorithms to receive travel plans tailored to your interests and feedback.
Community Engagement:
Encourage locals and travelers to share their insights and promote sustainable tourism practices.
Accessible Travel Information:
Leverage open data to provide comprehensive details on accessible travel options, including public transportation, accommodations, and attractions that cater to various mobility needs.
